Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 23, 2025

A BOUQUET OF BALLOONS LLC (“Company”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and services.

1. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information unless it is voluntarily provided (e.g., through the contact form). All information remains securely under your control.

2. Data We Do Not Access

  • Personal details such as name, email, or contact info (unless voluntarily submitted)

3. Local Device Storage

Any information you provide through our services remains secured and accessible only to you. We do not maintain centralized databases of sensitive user data.

4. No Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share or sell user data to advertisers, third-party services, or government entities.

5. Cookies

Our website may use minimal cookies for functionality and analytics. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or store information from children.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of any information you voluntarily provide to us. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@abouquetofballoons.com.

We may update this policy periodically. All updates will be posted here with the revised effective date.
