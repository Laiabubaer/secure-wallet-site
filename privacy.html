Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2025

Welcome to Bitcoin Wallet: BTC Star (“the App”), operated by the Bitcoin Wallet: BTC Star Team (“we”, “us”, or “our”). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information.

1. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All data remains securely stored locally on your device and is never shared with us or any third parties.

2. Data We Do Not Access

  • Personal details such as name, email, or contact info
  • Device identifiers (IP address, IMEI, MAC address)
  • Geolocation data
  • Wallet addresses, seed phrases, or transaction history

3. Local Device Storage

Your wallet data is encrypted and stored on your own device. You are fully responsible for protecting access to your device and any backups.

4. No Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share or sell user data to advertisers, third-party services, or government entities.

5. No Analytics or Tracking

The app does not use cookies, analytics tools, or tracking technologies.

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for users under the age of 13. If you believe a child has accessed the app, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not process or retain user data, you can delete your data by simply uninstalling the app from your device.

8. Legal Requests

As we do not hold any user data, we cannot provide any user information to law enforcement or other authorities.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. All updates will be posted here with an updated effective date.

10. Global Compliance

This policy is aligned with international privacy regulations, including GDPR and CCPA, by avoiding the collection or processing of personal data.
