1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At A BOUQUET OF BALLOONS, your privacy and security are our top priority. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data through the Secure Bitcoin Wallet. All data related to your use of the App, including Bitcoin wallet information or transaction details, is securely stored on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

We do not collect any personal information such as:

All data generated or used within the App remains exclusively on your device under your control.

3. How Is Your Data Stored?

Any data generated or managed by the App (e.g., wallet keys and transaction logs) is stored locally on your device. 4. No Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we do not share any information with third parties, including service providers, affiliates, or government entities, unless required by law (see Section 8).

5. Third-Party Services

Secure Bitcoin Wallet does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data. The App operates independently, ensuring that your information remains on your device.

The App is intended for general use and is not specifically directed at children. We do not knowingly collect or solicit information from users of any age, as no data is collected. If you have concerns about a child’s use of the App, please contact us at support@btcstarwallet.com.
